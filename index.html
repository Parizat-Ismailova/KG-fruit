Dried fruits - Dried fruits or berries from which most of the moisture found in fresh fruits
or berries has been removed by drying. Dried fruits are dried naturally (for example, through sun drying)
or using industrial methods(for example, through the use of speciallizad vegetable dryers or dehydrators).
Most dried fruits can be stored for 1 year at 60*F, 6 months at 80*F. Foods that are packaged seemingly
"bone dry" can spoil if moisture is reabsorded during storage.
Dried fruit can be preserved for much longer than fresh fruit and can be a handy snack,
particularly on long trips where refrigeration is not available.
